SPIDER THROWER

Spider Counter : 0

The rules and goal of this game are quite simple. Player Two Controls Bambi, the adorable Corgi who has found himself deep within a den of spiders. Bambi can be moved around with the arrow keys on your keyboard. Player One controls the ravenous arachnids which mercilessly descend upon Bambi by pressing the Q, W, E, R, and Space Keys. The game ends when Bambi falls prey to the spiders!
